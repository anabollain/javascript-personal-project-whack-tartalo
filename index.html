Tartalo

In Basque mythology, Tartalo is an enormously strong one-eyed giant very similar to the Greek Cyclops that Odysseus faced in Homer's Odyssey. He is said to live in caves in the mountains and catches young people to eat them; although he enjoys eating sheep too.
